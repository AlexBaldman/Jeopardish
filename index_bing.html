Jeopardish

Welcome to Jeopardish, a trivia game inspired by Jeopardy. To play, select a category and a difficulty level, then click on "Get Question". You will see a question and a text box to enter your answer. Click on "Submit Answer" to check if you are correct. You will see your score and feedback below. Have fun!
